A news-reactive research system for prediction markets.

The model considers several posteriors at every headline, weighs them against prior belief, and keeps the one evidence supports. A fractional-Kelly sizer then turns belief into a paper bet; every decision is logged for calibration.

the five-step loop - gemma 4 local

How shape thinks.

Here is what shape does, broken into five steps. It reads the news as it comes in, estimates how each story should move the market price, sizes a small paper bet, checks how the prediction held up, and uses what it got wrong to suggest the next thing to try.

  1. 01ingest

    Read the wire

    AP, Reuters and Bloomberg land locally, keyed to markets they might move.

  2. 02infer

    Branch and survive

    Gemma commits to a posterior and logs the alternatives it killed.

  3. 03size

    Fractional Kelly

    Edge becomes a quarter-Kelly paper stake with caps and abstentions.

  4. 04log

    Score calibration

    Every decision, fill and outcome gets scored by Brier buckets.

  5. 05review

    Self-review

    Autoresearch inspects misses and proposes the next backtest.

Zero server costs. Gemma runs locally via Ollama; positions are paper-only in this preview.Every branch and rejection stays inspectable.
